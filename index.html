About Me

My Introduction

 Since I was a child, I’ve love the art of creation. I’ve used to let my creative mind travel around all the possibilities of creating something innovative. This led me to study Computer Science and get involved with product design years later.

 As a product designer, I thoroughly enjoy the idea of solving real problems, to make an application useful, with clean design patterns and a great user experience.

 However, as a Frontend developer, I love to bring to life what were just ideas and make them accessible for everyone. I’ve been obsessed with the idea of perceiving programming as a way to realize all my inventions since my first code in Python.

 I consider myself an entrepreneurial-minded person, that being: well-organized, problem solver, adaptative and active feedback listener
